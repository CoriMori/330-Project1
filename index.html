Sand Art

Welcome to Sand Art! Click on the screen to drop sand on the canvas. You can change the color of the sand, the background color, the sand brush width, and even add walls to act as outlines. As is the nature of sand art, once placed down you cannot undo. You can clear the canvas if you wish to restart. When you are finished with you art, you can click export and save your art if you'd like.
